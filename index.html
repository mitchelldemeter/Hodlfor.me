Hodl now

We all know someone that has sold bitcoin or ethereum after some decent gains, or maybe at some point you've even sold some yourself. Imagine if you had held those 100 bitcoin, instead of selling at them at the then all time high of $100, $600 Or $5000. No more regrets. No more could of been. Start hodling now.

What is 'Hodling'?

'Hodling' is the act of holding a store of value like stocks or virtual currency.

Locked in

Our minimum term is 3 years. Once you give us the money, you're coins are purchased and secured by our team. No matter what happens, you can't access them for three years. You'll thank us later.

Steady growth

There has never been a good time to sell, so don't. Bitcoin has grown an average of 1195% year or year for the last 5 years. Ethereum has grown 5200% in the last year alone. Hodling avoids missing out on those gains.

Y tho?

Buying and securing crypto isn't easy, and you don't have time to become an expert. But that doesn't mean you should miss out on the gains. Give us your money and we will do it for you.

Sounds great, I'm in. Now what?

We've made this super simple, no need to understand what blockchains are or how mining works. Leave that to us. Do you understand exactly how email works? How about the internet? Most likely not and it doesn't matter. You don't need to understand the underworks of digital currencies to benefit.

  • It's never been easier.

    The process is simple. You give us your money, tell us if you'd like to Hodl Bitcoin or Ethereum. We buy it, secure it and hodl it.

  • What's the cost?

    We charge 5% on the way in and between 1-5% on the way out. Seems high? Go buy it somewhere else then, this non-negotiable.

  • Cashing out.

    Your funds are locked for three years, this is to prevent selling to early and missing out on gains. You get rewarded for hodling longer, selling after 3 years will cost 5% and after 5 years it's just 1%.

  • What if I die?

    When we're setting up your account and getting you started we're goingt to have some questions. This will be one of them. Tell us who you want us to give the riches to in the unfortunate case that something should happen to you. Have the peace of mind knowing your loved ones will be well taken care of.
